# Deliverables

The learner should produce:

- Group DNA principles.
- Leadership competency framework using Vision, Entrepreneurship, Passion, and Trust.
- Behavior indicators across emerging, proficient, and role-model levels.
- 360 feedback blueprint with raters, scale, confidentiality, and follow-up.
- Coaching program with coach profile, session cadence, and goals-to-habits plan.
- Talent mobility and succession readiness logic.
- Regional rollout and train-the-trainer plan.
- Measurement and adoption plan.

The AI co-workers should guide, critique, scaffold, and pressure-test. They should not write the final submission end-to-end.
