# Module 3: Regional Rollout and Adoption

The learner must cascade the leadership system across regions and brands without damaging brand identity.

Key decisions:
- Which region or market cluster pilots first?
- Which audience needs which message?
- Which channels and manager enablement steps are required?
- How can employees ask questions and influence the rollout?
- Which adoption KPIs prove the rollout is working?

Relevant agents:
- Regional Employer Branding & Internal Communications Manager owns rollout realism, localization, communication, trainer readiness, and employee voice.
- Gucci Group CHRO supports HR integrity and consistent competency definitions.
