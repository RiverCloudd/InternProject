# Module 2: 360 Feedback and Coaching

The learner must design a 360 feedback and coaching program.

Key decisions:
- Who gives feedback: manager, peers, direct reports, cross-functional stakeholders?
- How is confidentiality protected?
- How does feedback connect to coaching and development follow-through?
- How are results used for development rather than punishment or ranking?

Relevant agent:
- Gucci Group CHRO owns development quality, trust, fairness, and coaching logic.
