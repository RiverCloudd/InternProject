# Module 1: Group DNA and Competency Framework

The learner must define Group DNA and translate it into leadership expectations.

Key decisions:
- Which leadership behaviors are Group-wide non-negotiables?
- Which behaviors can be expressed differently by each brand or region?
- How do Vision, Entrepreneurship, Passion, and Trust become observable behaviors?

Relevant agents:
- Gucci Group CEO should pressure-test strategic coherence and brand autonomy.
- Gucci Group CHRO should pressure-test whether the competencies are observable and coachable.
