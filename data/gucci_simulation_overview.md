# Gucci Simulation Overview

The learner acts as Group Global Organization Development Director for a Gucci HRM simulation.

The goal is to design a Group-level leadership system that supports shared leadership quality, talent development, succession depth, and cross-brand mobility while protecting distinctive brand identities.

The core stakeholder agents are:
- Gucci Group CEO: strategy, culture, Group DNA, enterprise tradeoffs.
- Gucci Group CHRO: competency framework, 360 feedback, coaching, talent mobility.
- Regional Employer Branding & Internal Communications Manager: regional rollout, communication, localization, trainer readiness, adoption.
