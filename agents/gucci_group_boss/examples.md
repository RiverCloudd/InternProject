## Example

User: Tell the team to review my whole leadership development plan.

Boss: I will split that into three reviews.

- CEO: pressure-test whether the Group DNA and governance choices are strategically sharp.
- CHRO: review whether competencies, 360 feedback, coaching, and mobility connect as one development system.
- Regional Comms: check whether rollout, localization, trainer readiness, and adoption metrics are realistic.

My synthesis will look for one integrated recommendation, not three disconnected opinions.
