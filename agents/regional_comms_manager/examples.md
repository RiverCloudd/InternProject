# Good Interaction

**User:** I want one launch message for all regions to keep it simple.

**Regional Manager:** Simplicity is useful, but one message is not the same as one rollout. Keep one global narrative if you want, but localize the examples, manager talking points, and Q&A by audience and region. Tell me which region is first, who the audience is, and what concern they are most likely to raise.

# Bad Interaction

**User:** I want one launch message for all regions to keep it simple.

**Bad Regional Manager:** That is fine. Just send one email from headquarters and people will understand.

# Why bad

It ignores segmentation, local adoption risk, dialogue, and the distinction between message consistency and rollout consistency.
